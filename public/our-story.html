Our Story

Fine Jewelry Made With Intention

Vivato was born from a simple conviction: that luxury jewelry should never come at the cost of the planet or the people who inhabit it. We create heirloom-quality pieces set with certified lab-grown diamonds — stones that are chemically, physically, and optically identical to mined diamonds, grown with a fraction of the environmental footprint. Every ring, necklace, and earring we craft is an invitation to wear something beautiful that you can feel genuinely proud of. This is modern fine jewelry for buyers who want beauty, meaning, and better choices — without compromise.

Timeless Design Discipline

We draw on the enduring principles of fine jewellery — proportion, restraint, and craft — to create pieces that transcend trend cycles and become cherished for generations.

Responsible Sourcing, Always

Every diamond we use is lab-grown and independently certified. No conflict, no mining displacement — just exceptional stones held to the same grading standards as the world's finest natural diamonds.

Our Craft

From Stone to Story

Every Vivato piece follows a meticulous four-step journey — where luxury jewelry craftsmanship meets the precision of lab-grown diamond excellence.

01

Curate the Stone

Each lab-grown diamond is individually assessed against the highest grading standards — cut, clarity, colour, and carat — ensuring only exceptional stones earn a place in our collection. We source with intention, not volume.

02

Refine the Setting

Our master goldsmiths hand-craft each setting in recycled 18k gold or platinum. Every prong, pavé rail, and shank profile is shaped to complement the chosen stone — balancing structural integrity with the kind of delicate elegance found in the finest luxury jewelry.

03

Finish with Precision

Before any piece leaves our atelier, it undergoes a rigorous multi-point inspection. Prong security, surface polish, stone alignment, and overall symmetry are verified by hand and loupe — because a Vivato piece must be flawless at every angle.

04

Deliver with Confidence

Your piece arrives in signature Vivato packaging with a certified grading report, care guide, and our lifetime service promise. From the moment it leaves us to the moment it reaches you, every detail is handled with the care your investment deserves.

Our Craft

Built to Be Worn
for a Lifetime

Every Vivato piece is constructed with the same exacting standards found in the finest luxury jewelry — precision-set stones, hand-finished metals, and proportions refined for real-world comfort. We believe a milestone piece should feel as exceptional on the thousandth wear as it did on the first.

Solid Metal Quality

All settings are cast in solid 14k or 18k gold — white, yellow, or rose — and finished by hand. No hollow shanks, no plating. The metal you choose is the metal you wear, maintaining its integrity and warmth through decades of daily life.

Secure Stone Settings

Each lab-grown diamond is set using calibrated prong and bezel techniques that lock the stone firmly without obscuring its brilliance. Settings are inspected under magnification before leaving our workshop — because confidence should extend from how a ring looks to how it holds.

Designed for Lasting Wear

Band profiles are proportioned for comfort across extended wear — slightly rounded interiors, smooth edges, and weights balanced to sit naturally on the hand. Fine jewelry should never feel like an occasion. It should feel like yours.
