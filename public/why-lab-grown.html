Our Quality Standards

The 4Cs — Beauty You Can See, Quality You Can Trust

Every Vivato lab-grown diamond is graded by an independent third-party gemological laboratory — IGI or GIA — so you receive an unbiased certificate confirming exactly what you're buying. We apply the same exacting standards as the world's finest jewelers, ensuring every stone is set with master craftsmanship worthy of a lifetime.

Cut — Where Brilliance Is Born

Cut is the single most important factor in a diamond's visual impact. A precisely cut stone reflects light from every facet, producing the dazzling brilliance and fire you see when a diamond catches the light. We stock only Excellent and Very Good cut grades — the difference is immediately visible to the naked eye and defines the character of every piece we create.

Color — Pure, Icy Radiance

The color scale runs from D (perfectly colorless) to Z (warm yellow tint). Colorless and near-colorless diamonds in the D–H range allow the most light to pass through, amplifying brilliance and giving the stone that coveted bright, icy appearance. Vivato curates stones in the D–H range so your jewelry looks luminous in every light — natural, candlelight, or photography.

Clarity — Flawless to the Eye

Clarity measures the presence of natural inclusions or surface blemishes. Diamonds graded VS2 and above are considered eye-clean — meaning any tiny internal characteristics are invisible without magnification. You experience a stone that looks flawless in real life while staying within a sensible budget. Every Vivato diamond is eye-clean, so confidence comes standard.

Carat — Size That Makes a Statement

Carat refers to a diamond's weight, which directly relates to its size. Because lab-grown diamonds cost significantly less than mined alternatives, you can choose a larger carat weight without compromising on cut, color, or clarity. Vivato makes it possible to own the size you've always envisioned — ethically grown, certified, and set to perfection.

Your Questions, Answered

Everything You Need to Know About Lab-Grown Diamonds

We believe in full transparency. Here are honest, straightforward answers to the questions we hear most from our customers — so you can shop with complete confidence.

Are lab-grown diamonds as durable as mined diamonds?

Absolutely. Lab-grown diamonds are chemically, physically, and optically identical to mined diamonds — both score a perfect 10 on the Mohs hardness scale. They are just as resistant to scratching, chipping, and everyday wear, making them a lifetime choice.

Do lab-grown diamonds sparkle as brilliantly?

Yes — the sparkle, brilliance, and fire of a lab-grown diamond are indistinguishable from a mined stone. Because they share the same crystal structure and refractive index, even trained gemologists cannot tell the difference with the naked eye.

Are Vivato diamonds certified?

Every diamond we offer comes with an independent grading report from a recognised authority such as the IGI (International Gemological Institute) or GIA. Each certificate confirms the stone's cut, colour, clarity, and carat weight — giving you full, documented assurance of quality.

What should I expect for resale value?

We believe in honest guidance: like most fine jewelry, lab-grown diamonds are not a financial investment in the traditional sense. However, they allow you to purchase a significantly larger, higher-quality stone for the same budget — meaning more diamond, more brilliance, and more beauty from day one.

Are lab-grown diamonds a thoughtful gift?

They are an exceptional gift choice. Lab-grown diamonds carry the same emotional weight and visual splendour as mined diamonds, with the added reassurance that they are ethically sourced and conflict-free. Whether for a birthday, anniversary, or milestone, they make a meaningful and lasting impression.

Is a lab-grown diamond right for an engagement ring?

Without question. Lab-grown diamonds are the choice of a growing number of couples who want a stunning, conflict-free symbol of their commitment. Leading luxury jewelers and bridal designers now work exclusively with lab-grown stones — and your Vivato ring will be just as breathtaking as any mined-diamond alternative.
